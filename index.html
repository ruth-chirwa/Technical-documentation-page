RWD

Responsive web design is about creating a seamless experience across various devices and screen sizes.

By combining HTML, CSS, and JavaScript effectively, you can build flexible and adaptive websites that cater to a diverse audience. Consider testing your designs on different devices and browsers to ensure a consistent and user-friendly experience.
